Singapore Noodles

30 minutes

4 servings

How to Make It

- Cook the rice vermicelli noodles according to the package instructions (soak them in hot water for 3-4 minutes).
- Drain and set aside.
- In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, oyster sauce, rice vinegar, sugar, curry powder, and chili flakes. Set aside.
- Heat oil in a wok or large pan. Add shrimp, chicken, or tofu and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
- Add onions, garlic, bell pepper, and carrots to the pan, and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
- Push the vegetables to the side and scramble the eggs in the pan.
- Add the noodles to the pan and pour the sauce mixture over them. Toss everything together.
- Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes, add the cooked protein and bean sprouts, and mix well.
- Garnish with chopped green onions and serve with lime wedges.

Ingredients

  • 200g rice vermicelli noodles
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 200g shrimp, chicken, or tofu (optional)
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, julienned
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup curry powder
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)
