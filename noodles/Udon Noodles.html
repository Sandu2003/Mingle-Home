Udon Noodles

25 minutes

4 servings

How to Make It

- Cook the Udon noodles according to the package instructions (usually for 3-5 minutes in boiling water). Drain and set aside.
- Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat. Add garlic and onion, stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
- Add carrots, bell pepper, and mushrooms to the pan. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until vegetables begin to soften.
- Pour in the broth, soy sauce, and mirin (if using). Bring the mixture to a simmer.
- Add the Udon noodles to the pan and stir to combine. Let the noodles cook in the broth for 2-3 minutes, absorbing the flavors.
- Drizzle sesame oil and chili paste (if using) over the noodles and stir well.
- If using spinach or bok choy, add them to the pan and cook until wilted.
- Serve the Udon noodles hot, garnished with a boiled egg (optional), chopped green onions, sesame seeds, and lime wedges.

Ingredients

  • 300g Udon noodles
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 100g mushrooms, sliced (shiitake or button mushrooms)
  • 2 cups vegetable broth or chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp mirin (optional)
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp chili paste (optional)
  • 1/2 cup spinach or bok choy (optional)
  • 1 boiled egg, halved (optional)
  • 2 green onions, chopped for garnish
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds for garnish (optional)
  • Lime wedges for serving (optional)
