Pad Thai

30 minutes

4 servings

How to Make It

- Boil the rice noodles according to the package instructions (usually for 3-4 minutes).
- Drain the noodles and set them aside.
- Heat vegetable oil in a pan. Stir-fry garlic for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
- Add shrimp, chicken, or tofu and cook through.
- Scramble an egg in the pan, then add the noodles.
- Pour the sauce over the noodles and toss everything together.
- Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes, garnish with crushed peanuts and cilantro.
- Serve with lime wedges.

Ingredients

  • 2 packs Mie Goreng noodles
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu (optional)
  • 1/2 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1/2 cup carrots, julienned
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
  • 1 tsp sambal oelek (or chili paste, optional)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Cilantro or spring onions for garnish (optional)
  • Lime wedges for serving (optional)
