Rollo Cake

5 hours

10 servings

How to Make It

- Preheat the oven to 180Â°C (fan 160Â°C). Grease and line two 20cm round cake tins.
- Sift the flour, cocoa powder, and baking powder into a bowl.
- In another bowl, cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
- Beat in the eggs one at a time, then fold in the dry ingredients and milk alternately.
- Divide the mixture evenly between the prepared tins and bake for 25-30 minutes.
- Cool the cakes completely before assembling.
- For the frosting, beat butter, icing sugar, cocoa powder, and melted chocolate until smooth.
- Spread frosting between the cake layers and over the top and sides.
- Decorate with chocolate shavings or drizzle with melted chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 200g plain flour
  • 50g cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 200g caster sugar
  • 200g unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 eggs
  • 100ml milk
  • 200g icing sugar
  • 100g dark chocolate, melted
