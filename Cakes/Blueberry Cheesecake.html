blueberry Cheesecake

5 hours

8 servings

How to Make It

-Crush the biscuits into fine crumbs and mix
with melted butter.
-Press the mixture into the base of a 20cm
springform pan.
-Chill in the fridge for 30 minutes.
-Beat the cream cheese and sugar together until smooth.
-Add the double cream and vanilla extract, and beat
until thick and creamy.
-Fold half the blueberries into the cheese mixture.
-Spoon the mixture onto the chilled base and smooth the top.
-Gently heat the jam until loosened, then mix in the
remaining blueberries.
-Spread the blueberry mixture over the top of the cheesecake.
-Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to set.

Ingredients

  • 200g digestive biscuits
  • 100g butter, meltede
  • 500g cream cheese
  • 150g caster sugar
  • 200ml double cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 200g fresh or frozen blueberries
  • 2 tbsp blueberry jam
