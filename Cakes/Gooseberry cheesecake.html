Gooseberry Cheesecake

4 hours

8 servings

How to Make It

-Cook gooseberries with orange juice and elderflower cordial
for 10 minutes.
-Line a 23cm springform tin with parchment.
-Crush biscuits into crumbs, mix with melted butter, and press
into the tin. Chill for 30â€“60 minutes.
-Mix orange zest, mascarpone, soft cheese, icing sugar, and
remaining cordial.
-Fold in three-quarters of gooseberries and some cooking liquid.
Pour over the base.
-Chill for at least 4 hours or overnight.

Ingredients

  • 750g gooseberries
  • 4 tbsp elderflower cordial
  • 1 orange, zested and juiced/li>
  • 125g ginger biscuits
  • 125g digestive biscuits
  • 125g unsalted butter
  • 500g soft cheese
  • 250g mascarpone
  • 125g icing sugar
