Coconut Rice

25 minutes

4 servings

How to Make It

- Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain and set aside.
- In a medium-sized pot, heat oil or butter over medium heat. Add cumin seeds (if using), and sautÃ© for 30 seconds.
- Add chopped onion and garlic and cook until the onion becomes soft and translucent.
- Add the rice to the pot and stir for 1-2 minutes to coat the rice with the oil and spices.
- Pour in the coconut milk and water. Stir in a pinch of salt and bring to a boil.
- Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot with a lid, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
- Let the rice sit covered for 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork.
- Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve as a side dish or with your favorite curry.

  • 1 cup basmati rice (or preferred rice)
  • 1 Â½ cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped (optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish, optional)
