Paella Rice

45 minutes

4 servings

How to Make It

- In a small saucepan, bring the broth to a simmer. Add saffron threads and steep for a few minutes.
- Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onion and bell pepper, cooking for 3-4 minutes. Add garlic and cook for another minute.
- Stir in the rice and paprika, toasting it for 1-2 minutes.
- Add the wine (if using), then gradually add saffron-infused broth, stirring constantly, until the rice absorbs the liquid. Continue until rice is tender (about 18-20 minutes).
- Stir in peas and tomatoes. Add chicken and seafood during the last 5 minutes of cooking.
- Let the paella rest for 5-10 minutes before serving. Garnish with parsley and serve with lemon wedges.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice (or Paella rice)
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp saffron threads (optional)
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine (optional)
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken (optional)
  • 1/2 cup seafood (shrimp, mussels, etc., optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Lemon wedges for serving
