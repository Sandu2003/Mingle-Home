Sushi Rice

30 minutes

4 servings

How to Make It

- Rinse the sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear to remove excess starch.
- Cook the rice in a rice cooker or a pot with 2 cups of water. Once cooked, let it sit for 10 minutes.
- In a small saucepan, combine rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Warm until dissolved and then let it cool.
- Gently fold the vinegar mixture into the hot rice using a rice paddle or spoon. Avoid mashing the rice.
- Allow the rice to cool to room temperature before using it to make sushi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi or short-grain rice
  • 2 cups water (for cooking the rice)
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
