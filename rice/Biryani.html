Biryani

1 hours

5 servings

How to Make It

- Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes. Cook with spices until 70% done.
- Marinate the chicken with yogurt and spices for at least 30 minutes.
- Cook the marinated chicken with onions, tomatoes, and spices until partially cooked.
- Layer the rice over the chicken, sprinkle saffron milk, and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
- Garnish with fried onions, cilantro, and mint leaves. Serve hot with raita or salad.

Ingredients

For the Rice:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 2-3 green cardamom pods
  • 1-2 bay leaves
  • 1 cinnamon stick (1-inch piece)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 500g chicken pieces (bone-in or boneless)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

For the Biryani Masala:

  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 cup cilantro leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup mint leaves, chopped
  • Saffron strands (optional)
  • 2 tbsp milk (if using saffron)
