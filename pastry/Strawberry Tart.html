Strawberry Tart

1 hour 30 minutes

12 servings

How to Make It

- Preheat the oven to 350Â°F (175Â°C). Grease a tart pan with removable bottom. Mix the tart dough and refrigerate it for 30 minutes.
- Roll out the dough and fit it into the tart pan, then bake for 20-25 minutes until golden.
- Prepare the pastry cream by cooking milk, sugar, egg yolks, and cornstarch until thickened. Let it cool.
- Once the tart shell and pastry cream are cooled, spread the cream in the tart shell and arrange the sliced strawberries on top.
- Brush with apricot jam for a glossy finish. Refrigerate for at least an hour before serving.
