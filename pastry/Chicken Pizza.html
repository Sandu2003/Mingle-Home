Chicken Pizza

1 hours

4 servings

How to Make It

- Preheat the oven to 475Â°F (245Â°C).
- Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface into your desired shape and thickness.
- Place the dough on a greased baking sheet or pizza stone.
- Brush the dough with olive oil to prevent it from becoming soggy.
- Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
- Scatter the shredded mozzarella and cheddar cheeses evenly over the sauce.
- Add the shredded chicken, red onion, bell pepper, and mushrooms on top of the cheese.
- Sprinkle dried oregano and chili flakes (if using) over the toppings.
- Bake the pizza in the oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
- Remove the pizza from the oven and garnish with fresh basil leaves and Parmesan cheese (if desired).
- Slice and serve hot!

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 chicken breast, cooked and shredded
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/4 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)
  • 1 tbsp Parmesan cheese, grated (optional)
