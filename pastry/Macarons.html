Macarons

1 hour

2 servings

How to Make It

- Preheat the oven to 300Â°F (150Â°C) and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
- Pulse almond flour and powdered sugar, then sift. Whisk egg whites with granulated sugar until stiff peaks form.
- Fold dry ingredients into the egg whites, add food coloring if desired.
- Pipe 1.5-inch circles and tap the baking sheet to release air bubbles. Let sit for 30-60 minutes.
- Bake for 15-18 minutes. Let cool completely.
- Prepare buttercream or ganache and assemble the macarons by sandwiching the filling between shells.
- Refrigerate assembled macarons for 24 hours before serving.

Ingredients

For the Macaron Shells:

  • 1 3/4 cups (200g) powdered sugar
  • 1 cup (100g) almond flour
  • 3 large egg whites (at room temperature)
  • 1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar
  • Pinch of cream of tartar
  • Food coloring (optional)

For the Filling (Buttercream or Ganache):

  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1 1/2 cups (180g) powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp milk (or as needed)

    • OR for Ganache:


  • 4 oz (115g) dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter (optional for extra shine)
