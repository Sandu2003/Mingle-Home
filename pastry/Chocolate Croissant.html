Chocolate Croissant

25 minutes

6-8 servings

How to Make It

- Preheat your oven to 400Â°F (200Â°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
- Roll out the puff pastry sheet on a lightly floured surface.
- Cut the pastry sheet into triangles (about 6-8 pieces).
- Place a tablespoon of chocolate chips or chopped chocolate near the wide end of each triangle.
- Roll up the triangles from the wider end, making a croissant shape.
- Brush the tops with an egg wash for a golden finish.
- Bake for 15-18 minutes until golden and puffed.
- Optionally, sprinkle sugar on top for a sweet finish.
- Cool slightly before serving. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 sheet puff pastry (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips or finely chopped dark chocolate
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • 2 tbsp sugar (optional for topping)
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • A pinch of sea salt (optional)
