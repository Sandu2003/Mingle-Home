Eclair

1 hour 30 minutes

12 servings

How to Make It

-Preheat the oven to 375Â°F (190Â°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
-Make the choux pastry by boiling butter and water, adding flour, and mixing in eggs to create a smooth dough.
-Pipe dough onto the baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes until golden and puffed.
-For the pastry cream, cook milk, sugar, egg yolks, and cornstarch, then chill.
-For the glaze, melt chocolate and heavy cream together, then stir in butter.
-Once cooled, slice the eclairs, pipe in pastry cream, and dip in the chocolate glaze.
-Let the glaze set, and your delicious eclairs are ready to serve!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (240ml) water
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (125g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 4 large eggs
