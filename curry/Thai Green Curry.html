Thai Green Curry

40 minutes

4 servings

How to Make It

-Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
-Add the green curry paste and sautÃ© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
-Stir in chicken or tofu and cook until lightly browned.
-Add mixed vegetables, coconut milk, and chicken/vegetable stock.
-Add fish sauce, sugar, and kaffir lime leaves (if using).
-Simmer for 10-15 minutes until vegetables are tender and protein is cooked.
-Add fresh basil leaves, lime juice, and green chili (if desired).
-Serve hot with steamed rice and garnish with basil leaves.

Ingredients

  • 400g Chicken or Tofu (bite-sized)
  • 2 tbsp Thai Green Curry Paste
  • 1 tbsp Oil (vegetable or coconut oil)
  • 400ml Coconut Milk
  • 1 cup Chicken or Vegetable Stock
  • 1 cup Mixed Vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, zucchini, carrots)
  • 1 tbsp Fish Sauce (or Soy Sauce for vegetarian)
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • Fresh Basil Leaves (Thai basil preferred)
  • 1-2 Kaffir Lime Leaves (optional)
  • Juice of 1 Lime
  • 1 Green Chili (optional, for heat)
  • Steamed Rice (to serve)
