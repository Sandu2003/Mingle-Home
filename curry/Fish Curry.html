Fish Curry

45 minutes

4 servings

How to Make It

-Clean and marinate fish with turmeric, chili powder, and salt.
-Heat oil and sautÃ© fenugreek seeds (optional), then onions until golden.
-Add ginger-garlic paste and sautÃ© until aromatic.
-Mix in tomato puree, spices, and cook until oil separates.
-Pour in tamarind water and coconut milk; simmer.
-Add fish pieces, cover, and cook for 10-12 minutes.
-Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.

Ingredients

  • 500g Fish (cleaned and cut into pieces)
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1 large Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1/2 cup Coconut Milk
  • 1 cup Tamarind Water
  • Spices (Turmeric, Chili Powder, Cumin, Coriander)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Cilantro (for garnish)
