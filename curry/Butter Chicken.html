Butter Chicken

1 hours

5 servings

How to Make It

-Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices for 1 hour.
-Cook chicken until lightly browned, then set aside.
-SautÃ© onions in butter, then add tomato puree and spices.
-Add cashew paste, cooked chicken, and cream.
-Simmer for 10 minutes and garnish with cilantro.

Ingredients

  • 500g Boneless Chicken
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 large Onion (finely chopped)
  • 3 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 2-3 tbsp Cashew Nut Paste
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Cream
  • Spices (Turmeric, Chili Powder, Garam Masala, Cumin)
  • Salt to taste
