Iced Matcha Latte

5 min

1 servings

How to Make It

-Prepare the matcha.
-After making a paste with
matcha and water, whisk in additional water until frothy.
-You can use a standard whisk or the traditional bamboo whisk used for a cup of matcha.
-Put the sweetener in. Matcha and honey make a delicious flavor combination. You may also use maple syrup.
-For a change,consider using vanilla syrup instead!
-Make the milk foam. All you have to do is put it in a jar, cover it, and shake for 30 seconds.
-If you'd like, you can also omit this step.
-Add ice and mix. Pour in the ice, matcha, and frothed milk.
-You're ready for a latte!

Ingredients

  • green tea powder
  • Honey
  • Water
  • Milk
