Strawberry Lemonade Mocktail

5 min

1 serving

How to Make It

-In a blender, combine all the ingredients for the strawberry syrup and process until smooth.
-Add the lemon juice to the sparkling water. Fill a glass with ice,
-then add some strawberry juice and lemon sparkling water on top.

Ingredients

    Strawberry Syrup

  • 4 cups cut strawberries
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/4 cup honey

    • Lemon Sparkling Water

  • 1 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 3 cups sparkling water
