Pink Lemonade Moscato Punch

5 min

1 serving

How to Make It

-In two standard 16-cube ice cube trays, place two raspberries in each section.
-Pour cold water into the trays until they are three-quarters full.
-Freeze for at least 6 hours or overnight until solid.
-In the meantime, in a small pot, combine the sugar and 2/3 cup water. Over medium-high heat, bring to a boil, then lower the heat to a simmer and cook for 3 to 5 minutes, stirring regularly, until the sugar dissolves. Allow the simple syrup to cool fully before transferring it to a container and keeping it chilled.
-In a large pitcher, mix together the lemon juice and simple syrup until ready to serve. Stir the Moscato into the mixture. Fill six glasses with the Moscato mixture after dividing the ice cubes among them.
-Add the lemon rounds and mint sprigs as garnish.

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces raspberries
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 12 thin lemon rounds
  • One 750-milliliter bottle pink sparkling Moscato
  • 6 large mint sprigs
